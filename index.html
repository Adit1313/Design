OUR MISSION

Throughout the past century, we have seen revolution after revolution in every field, except Education. Here at Visualize, a group of like-minded, highly driven and passionate High School students aim to change that,

Technology has been the frontier of human development and growth. That is why we aim to make the learning more enriching my providing Augemented Reality experiences to every classroom.

THE IDEA

OUR PRODUCT

Our product aims to enrich the classroom in a way that has never been done before.

THE PROBLEM: Students struggle with 3D concepts such as DNA and 3D Motion Graphs. This leads them to search the web for long and boring explanations that don't aid them to learn visually. Eventually, there is a systemic barrier, the books, preventing the students to learn 3D concepts easily.

OUR SOLUTION: Here comes Visualize. We use the latest Augemented Reality technology to deliver complicated concepts directly to the students with the power of their mobile computing device, all with one easy-to-use application.
